Grid Marker

Tap cells, a row (R), or a line (L) to mark. See where they landed, with dates.
Mark numbers to begin.
Draws with your marks
How it works: the board is the 10×5 grid — number n sits at row (n-1)//5+1, line (n-1)%5+1. Tap a cell to mark it, or tap an R label to mark a whole row, or an L label for a whole line. Each cell shows its hit-count in the chosen window (redder = more). Marked numbers light gold, and every past draw they appeared in is listed below with its date — your marked numbers highlighted.
